Who We Are...

Drift Presets was created in 2011 with the goal of bringing photographers together to showcase their work and their editing tools. We want to help you bring your vision for your photography to life with the presets that suit your needs.

Our featured photographers have spent years honing their craft and perfecting their editing techniques. We bring that skill and knowledge straight to you with our curated preset packages.

We here at Drift believe in community and helping each other-- and we are always here to do just that. We are here to answer any questions you may have and to help you improve your personal editing experience. We look forward to hearing from you and to making great art together!
